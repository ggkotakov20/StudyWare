Lungs and Common Lungs Diseases:

The lungs are essential organs that help us breathe by taking in 
oxygen and getting rid of carbon dioxide. Here are some 
common lung diseases:

1. Asthma: A chronic condition that causes inflammation and narrowing
of the airways, making it difficult to breathe.

2. Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD): A group of lung diseases 
that make it hard to breathe due to damage to the airways and/or air 
Sacs in the lungs.

3. Pneumonia: An infection that inflames the air sacs in one or 
both lungs.

4. Tuberculosis (TB): A bacterial infection that primarily affects the 
lungs but can also affect other parts of the body.

5. Lung cancer: A condition where cells in the lungs grow abnormally 
and form tumors.
